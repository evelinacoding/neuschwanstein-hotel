49°DEUTSCH invites travelers to discover the essence of Germany through a seamless blend of heritage, adventure, and refined luxury. From the romantic castles of Bavaria to the vibrant streets of Berlin and the serene vineyards along the Rhine, guests are guided by local experts into authentic and breathtaking experiences. Whether it's hiking through the Bavarian Alps, cycling along the Moselle, skiing in the Black Forest, or gliding down the Danube on a private river cruise, every journey is designed to immerse guests in Germany's iconic landscapes. With access to luxury automobiles, curated excursions, and a collection of boutique hotels in historic towns and natural havens, 49°DEUTSCH offers elevated stays for the discerning traveler.


In addition to adventure and hospitality, 49°DEUTSCH curates moments that honor Germany's cultural heritage. Guests are invited to explore world-class museums, savor Michelin-starred dining rooted in local tradition, and engage in sustainable practices that support regional communities. By celebrating craftsmanship, history, and eco-conscious innovation, 49°DEUTSCH creates unforgettable experiences that connect travelers not only to place, but to the enduring spirit of Germany.
